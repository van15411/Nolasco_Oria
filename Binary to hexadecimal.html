Binary-Hexadecimal and vice versa





Converting binary to hexadecimal and vice versa is like the process of

converting binary to octal. The only difference is that in octal number system,

there are 3-Bit, while hexadecimal uses 4-Bit number.

EXAMPLE 1

Binary to Hexadecimal

Convert 111100012 to hexadecimal

(1111) = F

(0001) = 1

Therefore conclude that 111100012 = F116



EXAMPLE 2

Hexadecimal to Binary

Convert 9A16 into binary.

(9) = 1001

(A) = 1010

Therefore conclude that 9A16 = 100110102
